Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Last Updated: November 2025

We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and store your information.

1 Data We Collect

Profile Information

What: Name, email, skin type, concerns, and goals

Purpose: Essential for app functionality

Profile Pictures

What: Images you upload for your profile

Purpose: Stored securely in Firebase Storage

Usage Data

What: App usage patterns and preferences

Purpose: Helps improve app experience

Device Information

What: Device type, operating system, app version

Purpose: Technical support and compatibility

2 How We Use Your Data

Personalized Experience

Customize recommendations based on your skin profile

App Functionality

Enable core features like routine tracking

Improvements

Analyze usage to improve app features

Support

Provide customer support and assistance

3 Your Rights Under GDPR

Right to Access

Request a copy of all your personal data

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data

Right to Object

Opt out of certain data processing

4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • We only collect data necessary for functionality

5 Regional Compliance

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation. You have enhanced privacy rights and additional data protection measures.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

For California residents, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act. You have specific rights regarding your personal information.
